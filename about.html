About Microsoft

Our mission is to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more.

Our Mission

At Microsoft, we strive to create technology that is accessible to everyone, everywhere. We believe in the power of innovation to transform lives and drive progress.

Our History

Founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen, Microsoft has grown from a small startup to one of the world's most influential technology companies. Over the decades, we've revolutionized the way people work, play, and connect.

Our Values

We are committed to diversity, inclusion, and sustainability. Our values guide everything we do, from developing innovative products to supporting communities around the world.
